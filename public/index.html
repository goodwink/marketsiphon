Referral marketing made easy.

Market Siphon makes it easy for any site to increase traffic and conversions with sponsored links. You only pay for traffic that converts to sales. There's no special codes for affiliates to add to links, so it just works.

Get started »

1. Setup Your Site

All you have to do to get started is paste small bits of text we'll give you into your landing page and your conversion page. Affiliates can use normal links with no special affiliate codes.

2. A Visitor Follows a Link to Your Site

When a visitor follows a link to your site from an affiliate, Market Siphon records the click and then waits to see if the new visitor converts to a sale.

3. The Visitor Converts to a Sale

When a visitor from an affiliate completes a sale or visits any page you choose as a conversion page Market Siphon records the conversion and credits the affiliate's account.

Payday!

Market Siphon takes care of collecting the commissions from advertisers and paying affiliates. You don't have to manage your affiliates and you'll only have one, consolidated payment.
